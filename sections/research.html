ArXiv Preprint

Brain Tumor Diagnosis Using Quantum Convolutional Neural Networks

Presents a Hybrid Quantum Convolutional Neural Network (HQCNN) integrating quantum feature-encoding circuits with depth-wise separable convolutional layers for MRI-based brain tumor classification. Achieved 99.16% training accuracy and 91.47% validation accuracy. Quantum layers capture complex non-linear relationships while separable convolutions ensure computational efficiency, keeping the architecture compatible with near-term quantum hardware.

M. A. Khan, N. Innan, A. A. Galib, and M. Bennai. ArXiv, 2024.
